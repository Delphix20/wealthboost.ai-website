Questions, answered

Common questions about WealthBoost AI and how it helps you build wealth daily.

Is WealthBoost AI financial advice?

WealthBoost AI provides educational coaching and habit‑building. It helps you take practical steps in money, earning, and savings with clear daily guidance.

How are the tasks personalized?

Each task is tailored based on info from your onboarding profile, goals, time, skills, focus, and feedback. They stay short, realistic, and balanced so you can actually finish them.

Will I get the same tasks every day?

No. The goal is variety without randomness. New daily content is generated using your profile and recent activity to reduce repetition while still keeping the tasks relevant and engaging.

What do I do if I’m busy?

Do at least one task each day. That’s enough. The app is designed so small wins still count and momentum stays alive.
